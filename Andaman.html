Andaman witnessed the first sunrise of the millennium. An island that captures misty, amber reflections of the sun, soaks grainy sand in its crystal clear waters, gives birth to outrageous volcanoes and seeps in the roots of the dense woodland, Andaman holds the pure essence of nature and how! This gem gives other island destinations a run for their money. Coral reefs, glimmering shores, far-stretched with white sands and tall salt-tolerant mangroves along the coastline; it is the only island in India that has gained immense commercial popularity in the last few decades, yet delivers the old island charm. Port Blair, Havelock islands, Long Island, Rangat Island and Neil Island are some of the popular tourist destinations in Andaman and hail many beautiful beaches that are pure bliss
